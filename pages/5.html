In case you have only VIRTUAL CARD and now you want to use it on another device, please make sure to deactivate your card on your current device first before installing signing in another device. If your account or phone is stolen, you can contact us at 086 22 32 62 (telegram / whatsapp is available) / 023 214 089 for support.

If your REAL CARD is lost or stolen, you can deactivate it by using the BROWN Coffee App or the BROWN website. Once you deactivate your card, we will freeze your balance and points so you may later activate if you find your card.

If you lose and cant find your card, you may purchase a new one to renew your account. The balance and points in your previous card will be transferred onto your new card.
