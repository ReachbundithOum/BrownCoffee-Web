Go Cashless, Go Card-less, GO CONTACTLESS with BROWN APP!

With BROWN APP, getting a perfect cup of coffee at BROWN is super convenient. You can pay for all your transactions using just your phone whether it’s in store, delivery services, or self-pick-up.

For transaction in store, you just need to show the QR/CODE on your phone to our cashier by following three steps below:

  1. Tab on “Pay” on the task bar
  2. Confirm your Finger Print/Face ID or Pin code
  3. Show or Read the Digit Code to our cashier

Delivery transaction: Follow the instruction below and our delivery man will bring all you need to you right at your door for free and with a smile.

  1. Tab on “Delivery”,
  2. Pin location you need us to deliver to on the map, or in put house number, street number then tab on “Continue”
  3. Adding any items on the menu to CART by tabbing on (+) sign next to each menu and modify each item, then tab on “CART” on the top right to confirm your order.
  4. Choose payment option then tab on “ Check out ”​
  5. Tab on “Pay”

Self Pick Up transaction:

  1. Tab on “Self Pick Up”
  2. Select store to pick your order then tap on “Continue”
  3. Adding any items on the menu to CART by tabbing on (+) sign next to each menu and modify each item, then tab on “CART” on the top right to confirm your order.
  4. Choose payment option then tab on “ Check out ”
  5. Select time to pick up then tab on “Check out” then “Pay”
