With BROWN Card on your phone or real card in your hand, you can easily top up your balance by your own self through BROWN app or our cooperation bank or by simply visiting any of the BROWN stores. You can reload your balance as little as 5 USD or as much as 100 USD or more per top-up transaction.

  1. On Card page tap on “Top-up”
  2. Choose amount to top up or enter preferred amount ( 5 USD - 100USD)
  3. Choose cooperated bank
  4. Tap on “Pay”
  5. Link to cooperated bank then tap on “Pay Now”

All amounts loaded into your BROWN Card are held and denominated in US Dollars. You can use your balance to pay for any goodies in BROWN store including drink, home-made pastry & cake, comfort food, coffee bean and merchandise.
