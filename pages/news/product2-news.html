Taste and look of our new breakfast this time, we guarantee you will make an order for a second time.

Beef Quesadillas is a new breakfast having beef, vegetable and cheese wrapped in crispy tortilla wrap. Our new Chicken Shakshuka is a hot dish with a sweet & sour taste baked with tomato. The dish is exceptional when you have it with our crunchy home-made bread.

Available at all BROWN stores, except BROWN DTT and BROWN Seaview.

Note: Chicken Shakshuka available at BROWN Roastery only.
