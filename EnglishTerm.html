Topping Up the Brown Gift Card

The Cardholder can top up a dollar value onto the Brown Gift Card by visiting any Brown Coffee store with the option of $5, $10, $15, and $ 20 worth of value. The maximum and minimum amount of the top-up may change from time to time under the sole discretion of Brown Coffee and shall be notified to each Cardholder at the point of sale (POS) or through leaflet notice in Brown Coffee Store or by phone when you call or on our website.

All amounts loaded onto your Brown Gift Card are held and denominated both Riel (Cambodian Currency) and US Dollars. Currency conversions are based on currency exchange rates applicable on the date of the transaction set by Brown Coffee.

Any future updates of the top-up method will be posted in our Mobile App and our website.

The Brown Gift Card and its account balance has no expiration date and any changes or upgrading will inform the cardholders.
